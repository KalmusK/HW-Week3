Pizza

Pizza has always had a place in our lives. Fresh hot pizza out of oven is not just fill our stomach. It can warm our hearts from the inside. It really does not matter if it is hot or cold, it'll always be there for us. Everyone likes it different, some people even put pineapple on it.

Ingredients Amount
Water 600ml
Pizza flour 900g
sugar 1 teaspoon
salt 1 teaspoon
Tomato sauce 800g
Cheddar As much as you like
Pepperoni 300g
Minced meat 300g
  1. Mix the flour with water until a nice dough is made
  2. Add salt and sugar
  3. The dough should be soft and smooth
  4. Pour the dough onto the baking pan
  5. Fry the minced meat
  6. Preheat the oven ~230ËšC
  7. Add tomato sauce onto the dough
  8. Add minced meat
  9. Add pepperoni
  10. Add cheese
  11. Bake pizza in the oven until it's nicely baked
  12. Cheese and crust should be golden brown
  13. ENJOY!
